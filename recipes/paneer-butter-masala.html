Paneer Butter Masala

Silky tomato-cashew gravy with soft paneer cubes. The ultimate comfort dish loved across India and beyond.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak cashews in warm water for 15 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onion and cook until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Cool and blend into a smooth puree.
  5. Return the puree to the pan. Add cashew paste, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Finish with fresh cream and garam masala. Garnish with coriander.
  8. Serve hot with naan or jeera rice.
Tip: For a vegan version, use tofu and cashew cream.
