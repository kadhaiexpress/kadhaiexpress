Key Observations (2026):
  • India remains the global leader in both percentage and absolute numbers of vegetarians.
  • Plant-based market growth is strong but India’s plant-based packaged food market is still emerging compared to its traditional home-cooked vegetarian base.
  • Health, sustainability, and flexitarian trends are accelerating adoption in urban India and globally.
